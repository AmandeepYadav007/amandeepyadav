Who I Am

I am Amandeep Yadav, a BBA student at Mahindra University's School of Management, specialising in Computational Business Analytics. My work sits at the intersection of quantitative analysis and strategic business thinking — where data stops being a report and becomes a competitive weapon.

My academic journey has been defined by a drive to operate at the highest level. I earned a Merit Certificate from the National University of Singapore in AI-Powered Business Analytics — an internationally recognised programme where I mastered enterprise-grade tools, built BI dashboards, and processed real-world datasets to simulate executive decision-making scenarios.

Beyond the technical, I've demonstrated operational leadership at scale. As Event Head for the Bharat Higher Education Summit, I directed a national-level academic forum that convened Vice Chancellors, Deans, and senior administrators from institutions across India — managing high-stakes logistics, stakeholder relations, and flawless protocol execution.

I currently serve as Captain of the Mahindra University Football Team — a role that demands the same clarity of vision, composure under pressure, and command of team dynamics that I bring to every professional engagement.

I am actively seeking internship and entry-level roles in consulting, finance, and data-driven marketing where analytical rigour meets strategic impact.
